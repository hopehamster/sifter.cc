Physical reality.

Most apps pull you away from the world. Sifter pulls you deeper into it.

If you aren't here, you aren't in.

Chat rooms are tied to physical coordinates. Walk in to join. Walk out to leave. No remote lurkers allowed.

Zero Footprint.

No chat logs. No history tracking. Conversations exist only in the moment, just like real life.

No Friction.

Forget QR codes, invites, or friend requests. Just open the app and talk to the room immediately.

1. Enter a Zone

Sifter detects public spaces and events around you. No setup required—just walk into the geofence.

2. Join the Conversation

See who's there. Ask questions, share updates, or just banter. Everyone in the chat is physically present.

3. Leave Without a Trace

Walk away, and the chat disappears. No notifications following you home. Your privacy is guaranteed.
