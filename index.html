Frequently Asked Questions

The short version of what Sifter is, how it works, and what makes it different.

What is Sifter?

Sifter is a location-based chat app for iPhone. Users create chat rooms anchored to real-world places, and other people who are physically within the room's geofence can see and join the conversation. When you leave the area, you leave the chat.

How is Sifter different from a regular chat app?

Every chat room is tied to a precise geographic boundary. You can only see and participate in rooms that you are physically standing inside. There is no remote access, no global feed, and no random pairing. Every conversation is grounded in a real place.

Is Sifter free?

Yes. Sifter is free to download on the Apple App Store and free to use. No subscription required.

Do I need an account to use Sifter?

Yes. Creating chat rooms requires a verified account — a phone number with SMS verification and a username. Some rooms allow guest access, which still requires a verified phone number but lets you appear with a limited profile.

Does Sifter share my location with other users?

No. Your precise location is used only to determine which chat rooms you are inside the boundary of. Sifter never shares your coordinates, your address, or your movements with other users.

What iOS version do I need?

Sifter requires iPhone running iOS 15.6 or later.
