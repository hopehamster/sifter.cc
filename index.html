Frequently Asked Questions

The short version of what Sifter is, how it works, and what makes it different.

What is Sifter?

Sifter is a chat-room app for iOS where every chat is tied to a specific physical place. When you walk into a bar, a festival, or a hotel lobby, you can open Sifter and join the conversation already happening there — or start one for the people around you. The chat exists for where you are, when you are there.

Who is Sifter for?

Sifter is for anyone in a place where they'd want to talk to the people physically around them but can't — Vegas tourists in a hotel lobby, festival-goers between sets, conference attendees in a hallway, college students on campus. Free on iOS 16 or later.

How is Sifter different from Yik Yak?

Yik Yak was anonymous with a wide radius — that combination made it unsafe. Sifter authenticates every user (Apple Sign-In or phone) and lets the user pick the radius for their chat room (one venue, one corner, one floor). Authenticated users plus user-defined geofence makes open conversation safe.

How is Sifter different from a dating app?

Dating apps assume romantic intent up front. Sifter doesn't. The chat room exists for whoever's physically present, conversation is open-ended, and the app handles no follow-up after you leave the venue. People sometimes meet on Sifter the way they sometimes meet at a bar — incidentally, not by design.

How does Sifter handle privacy?

Location is the privacy filter. Only people physically inside a venue's geofence can see or join its chat room. When you leave, you leave the chat. Sifter knows who you are (authentication is required), but conversations are tied to physical co-presence — not stored, not searchable, not forwarded outside the room.

What does Sifter cost?

Sifter is free on the iOS App Store. No in-app purchases, no ads inside chat rooms, no premium tier at launch. The app requires iOS 16 or later. Android is in development; sign up at sifter.cc to be notified when it launches.

Where can I download Sifter?

Sifter is on the iOS App Store at sifter.cc/download. The download link redirects to the official App Store listing. iOS 16 or later required.
