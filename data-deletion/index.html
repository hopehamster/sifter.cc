Account & Data Deletion

Last Updated: April 2026

To delete your Sifter account and all associated data, use one of the following methods:

  • In the app: Settings → Account → Delete Account in the Sifter iOS app
  • By email: Send an email to info@sifter.cc with "Delete Account" in the subject line

Deletion is processed within 30 days of the request.
